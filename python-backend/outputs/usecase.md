- **Service‑Based Revenue:** Income is generated from contracts for **Precision Crop Logistics**, **Soil & Science Consulting**, and **Agro‑Tech Integration**. Each contract likely follows a project‑or subscription‑style billing cycle aligned with the phased rollout.  
- **Phase‑Linked Upsell Path:**  
  - *Phase 01 IoT Expansion* creates a data‑collection layer that can be monetized through sensor‑as‑a‑service fees.  
  - *Phase 02 AI Analytics* adds a predictive analytics subscription, potentially tiered by data volume or advisory depth.  
  - *Phase 03 National Logistics* introduces a logistics‑service fee structure for cold‑chain transport and distribution.  
- **Lead Generation Funnel:** The home‑page contact form captures qualified leads; subsequent engagement (e.g., roadmap review) moves prospects deeper into the sales funnel, increasing conversion likelihood for higher‑value phases.

- **Data Privacy Concerns:** Inadequate communication of privacy practices could reduce form submissions.  
- **Regulatory Changes:** New agricultural transport regulations could affect Phase 03 logistics contracts.  

Mitigation strategies should focus on targeted education (e.g., case studies on the home page), transparent data handling statements, and proactive regulatory monitoring.
